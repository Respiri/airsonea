How It Works

Use AirSonea anywhere to take and accurate measurement of your WheezeRATE™.
AirSonea syncs with your smartphone and transmits data about wheezing symptoms to AsthmaSense via Bluetooth®. Data are transmitted to the cloud where other critical information is kept and used to help calculate your risk of an asthma attack.
